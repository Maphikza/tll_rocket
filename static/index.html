The Financial Intelligence Centre Act, 2001

The Act seeks to establish a Financial Intelligence Centre and a Counter-Money Laundering Advisory Council to combat money laundering activities and the financing of terrorist and related activities. It also imposes certain duties on institutions and other persons who might be used for money laundering purposes and the financing of terrorist and related activities.

1A. Application of Act when in conflict with other laws Back to Top
